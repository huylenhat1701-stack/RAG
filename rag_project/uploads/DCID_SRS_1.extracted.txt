5. CAC YEU CAU PHI CHUC NANG (NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS)
5.1 Hieu nang (Performance)
Thoi gian phan hoi API trung binh < 200ms cho cac truy van thong thuong.
Ho tro it nhat 100 nguoi dung dong thoi tren cau hinh VPS tieu chuan.
He thong WebSocket dam bao do tre cap nhat trang thai ho so < 1 giay.
5.2 Bao mat (Security)
Ma hoa du lieu nhay cam trong co so du lieu (AES-256 hoac tuong duong).
Su dung HTTPS/TLS 1.2+ cho toan bo cac ket noi ngoai vi.
Co che phan quyen chat che (RBAC) duoc quan ly tap trung tai IAM (Keycloak).
Xac thuc 2 lop (2FA/TOTP) bat buoc doi voi tai khoan Can bo va Admin.
Quet lo hong bao mat dinh ky (OWASP Top 10).
5.3 Tinh san sang (Availability)
He thong giam sat (Prometheus & Grafana) canh bao tuc thoi khi co su co tai cac service (Kafka, DB, Backend).
Muc tieu uptime >= 99.5% theo thang.
Co che tu dong khoi phuc (auto-restart) khi container bi loi qua Docker health check.
5.4 Kha nang bao tri (Maintainability)
Ma nguon tuan thu cac quy tac Clean Code va duoc kiem tra boi unit test.
Tai lieu API duoc tao tu dong tu OpenAPI/Swagger.
Trien khai CI/CD de tu dong hoa qua trinh build, test va deploy.

6.2 Cac thuc the chinh (Core Entities)
User: Thong tin tai khoan, vai tro va quyen han (lien ket voi Keycloak).
Application: Ho so hanh chinh, loai thu tuc, trang thai hien tai.
StatusHistory: Lich su thay doi trang thai ho so (audit trail).
Procedure: Cau hinh JSON Schema cho moi loai thu tuc hanh chinh.
Attachment: Thong tin file dinh kem, duong dan MinIO, hash kiem tra toan ven.
Notification: Lich su thong bao da gui qua cac kenh Email va FCM.
6.3 Nguyen tac thiet ke CSDL
Ap dung chuan hoa du lieu (3NF) cho PostgreSQL.
Moi bang chinh deu co truong created_at, updated_at va soft-delete (deleted_at).
Du lieu nhay cam (so CMND, so dien thoai) duoc ma hoa truoc khi luu.
Chinh sach backup: full backup hang ngay, incremental backup moi 6 gio.
